The problem

Independent P&C is large, fragmented, and stuck.

  • Thousands of independent agencies running service on legacy AMSs and tribal knowledge.
  • Service is the largest cost line and the least measured.
  • Every operator is under pressure to use AI, with no foundation to run it on.

Market-size and fragmentation figures to be sourced from a citable industry report before external use.

The solution

The operating stack, on top of what agencies already run.

Three layers, plus the AI and tooling that run across them. Each is a wedge into a fragmented market, and a source of advantage that compounds as it scales.

The three layers
01

Connect

Data plane

Makes the book usable across every AMS: consolidates records, resolves duplicate accounts, and feeds everything from one trusted source.

The wedge into a fragmented market. Once COVU is the connective layer across systems that were never built to talk to each other, it becomes the trusted source of record for service, earned without lock-in and harder to displace as more books and carriers connect.

Explore Connect →
02

OS

Control plane

Decomposes service into tasks, routes them by license and skill, and measures what each one costs.

Turns invisible, tribal-knowledge work into structured, measurable data. That foundation is what everything else compounds on: pricing, automation, and margin all improve as the task base grows. It is the layer that makes the operation legible enough to run like a business.

Explore OS →
03

Service Network

Supply layer

On-demand capacity to do the work: U.S. licensed agents, vetted capacity, and VERO AI, all routed and governed through OS.

Capacity that scales without agencies hiring. As AI absorbs more routine work, human effort moves to higher-value tasks and the economics improve with volume, turning service from a fixed headcount cost into flexible, revenue-aligned supply.

Explore the Service Network →
Across the stack
AI

VERO

AI across the stack

COVU's AI, working inside Connect, OS, and the Service Network. It triages, drafts, and executes service work on a standardized foundation.

The compounding advantage. Because the work is structured first, AI compounds here instead of plateauing the way bolted-on pilots do. Every task the stack runs makes it better, on top of operating data that is not available to anyone building from the outside. This is where the long-term margin story lives.

Explore VERO →
EXT

BEX

Browser extension

Captures task-efficiency data as agents work in the browser, and lets them send work to COVU from inside the AMS or email.

Extends the operating data COVU can see and act on into the tools agencies already live in. It lowers the friction of adoption and widens the data advantage with every action it observes, reaching agencies long before they migrate anything.
