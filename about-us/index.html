The origin

We Lived the Problem First.

Before COVU was a product, we'd been running and scaling P&C agencies ourselves. We sat in the owner's seat, carried the payroll, and watched service quietly become the largest cost line and the least visible part of the business.

We tried what everyone tries — more CSRs, off-the-shelf tools, offshore labor. None of it fixed the real problem: service work that no one could see, standardize, or scale. So we built the system we wished we'd had.

“We didn't set out to make software. We set out to fix the agencies we were running and scaling — and the software is what it took.”

1

We ran the agencies

Founded by industry operators, we managed real books of business and $200M+ in premium — living every service, renewal, and endorsement firsthand.

We hit the wall everyone hits
