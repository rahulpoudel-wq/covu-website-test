The problem

Every carrier is its own island.

A service team lives between systems all day. That gap is where the errors, reworks, and callbacks start, and no AMS report ever sees it.

01

A service team juggles dozens of carrier portals, each with its own login and two-factor prompt.

02

The same facts, name, policy number, address, get re-keyed across portal after portal, and back into the AMS.

03

Copy and paste is most of the day. It is where errors, reworks, and callbacks start.

04

None of it shows up in AMS reporting, because it happens between the systems.
