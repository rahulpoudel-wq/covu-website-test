Service is the last part of your agency you can't measure.

You know your revenue, your pipeline, your loss ratios. But the work that eats most of your team's day runs on gut — no cost per task, no enforced standard, no way to see or shape it.

Every line but one has a number. Service — most of your team's day — is the black box.

No price on the work

You know total payroll — not what a COI or a renewal actually costs. So you can't find the expensive workflows or price service to profit.

Work goes to whoever, not whoever's right

Requests land on the account owner regardless of skill or license. Your best people do $12 tasks; licensed work lands on unlicensed desks.

The standard lives in people's heads

SOPs are a doc nobody follows. Every rep works their own way, quality drifts, and rolling out a change takes quarters.

You can't improve what you can't measure

With no cost, time, or quality per task, you can't tell if a change helped, spot the bottleneck, or A/B a workflow.

The knock-on So every bit of growth means more headcount, not more margin.

The AMS stores the record. COVU OS runs the work: it decomposes requests into tasks, routes them, enforces SOPs, and measures cost. It also gives teams one place to communicate with customers and external stakeholders while handling day-to-day service tasks.

COVU OS turns requests into ordered steps using agency instructions, best practices, and patterns from insurance transactions. A COI often runs as 6 standardized tasks, but depending on the agency and its SOPs, it may run as 7 or 8.

Tasks are routed by license, skill, eligibility, and capacity.

COVU OS tracks live ops, dashboards, reports, throughput, queue depth, cost per task, handle time, and audit history.
