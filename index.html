Does COVU replace my AMS?+
No. COVU runs on top, with two-way integration. Your AMS stays the system of record, and COVU makes the work in it usable, routable, and measurable.
Does my team get replaced?+
No. COVU takes the low-value, repeatable work off your best people so they can spend time selling and advising. Your team keeps the client relationships.
Is everything auditable?+
Yes. Every task carries a full audit trail, so you can see who did what, when, at what cost, and against which SLA.
Who actually does the work?+
A mix of U.S.-based licensed agents, vetted servicing capacity, and AI, routed by license and skill. You pay per task, only when it is complete.
