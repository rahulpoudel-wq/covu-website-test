What does AI enablement mean for a P&C agency?+
AI enablement means giving AI the operational foundation it needs to work in production, not just in a demo. Many AI tools can create an impressive one-off result, but they struggle when agency systems, data, and workflows are disconnected. COVU provides that connected foundation through the COVU Stack, so AI can support more parts of your operation over time.
What needs to be in place before AI can safely help with service work?+
Your systems need to work together, your workflows need structure, and your data needs to connect to frontier AI models in a controlled way. COVU connects those pieces so AI can operate inside the workflow instead of sitting off to the side.
Which tasks can AI help with first?+
Vero can triage inbound work, filter noise, route requests, draft responses, clean data, and support routine task completion.
How does COVU keep AI work supervised and auditable?+
Vero runs inside the COVU Stack with license gates, confidence thresholds, and an audit trail on every AI action, so the work stays controlled, reviewable, and measurable.
